Our Story

About LIB TUNEZ

LIB TUNEZ was born from one simple belief — Liberian music deserves a stage the world can hear. We built a platform where local artists can grow their craft, connect directly with fans who genuinely care, and earn the support they deserve. Whether you're a bedroom producer in Monrovia or a vocalist touring the continent, LIB TUNEZ is your home base — the place where the Liberian sound meets a global audience.
